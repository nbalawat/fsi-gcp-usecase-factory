Option C · affordance

↗ open standalone
density ★★★★☆ motion · minimal aff · inline-per-section metaphor · annotated-document persona · BSA Analyst writing the narrative

Option C treats the SAR draft as an ANNOTATED DOCUMENT. The narrative is the page; every claim is one paragraph with an inline-citation group at the end of its prose; each citation chip is a tight mono affordance ([TXN 04891], [GEO MX], [AGT categorizer], [SVC peer], etc.) that drives a sticky right-rail EvidenceDrawer where the source record appears. Below every claim sit three inline actions - flag this, dispute this, add note - so the analyst can dispose of disagreement without leaving the prose. The /approval/[id] route renders the same annotated narrative with the shared ApprovalGate prim

Filing the SAR is freezing the sub-graph and signing off through the shared ApprovalGate primitive, rendered inline below the narrative on the approval route.
